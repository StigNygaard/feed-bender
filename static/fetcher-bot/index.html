Feed-bender - About the fetcher bot

Feed-bender is a personal project to create, tweak or combine various news feeds (sources) into "custom feeds" for use in my newsreader or as "data-feeds" for use in personal hobby-projects. But the created "custom feeds" are public and free for others to use if anybody finds them valuable.

The Feed-bender fetcher bot usually shouldn't visit each source-feed more than once per hour, but in periods of experimentation and new developments, the frequency might temporarily be higher.

The fetcher bot currently doesn't know/check the content of robots.txt files. If the bot is creating trouble on your site, please contact me directly. I might have a profile on the site that the bot is crawling, that I can be contacted via. Other options include:
