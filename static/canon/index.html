Canon Rumors - Essential posts only

Lately Canon Rumors has become a very spammy site, with lots of irrelevant and low quality posts to attract "random" searchers and get more "affiliate clicks". Especially annoying if you follow site-updates via Canon Rumors' official RSS news feed (or newsletter). But there are still interesting posts I want to see, I just don't want my news-reader flooded with all the other noise. So I have tried creating a slimmed-down custom news-feed with only "interesting" posts.

"My feed" is based on the official news feed, but I'm filtering it in an attempt to keep the essential posts only. Currently the feed skips posts in the categories Deal Zone, Buyers Guide, Smart Picks, Canon Reviews and Industry News. But I will adjust the filtering when I feel the need to do so.
