Canon Rumors - Essential posts only

Lately Canon Rumors has become a very spammy site, with lots of irrelevant and low quality posts to attract "random" searchers and get more "affiliate clicks". But among all the noise, there are still relevant and interesting posts. I just don't want my news reader flooded with all the other crap. So I have created a slimmed down news feed.

"My feed" is based on the official news feed, but I'm filtering it in an attempt to keep the essential posts only. For now, the feed omits posts in the categories Deal Zone, Buyers Guide, Smart Picks and Industry News. But I might of course adjust that as needed.
