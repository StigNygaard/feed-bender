Canon Rumors - Essential posts only

Especially if you follow Canon Rumors via their own official RSS news feed (or their newsletter), it has lately become a very "spammy" site, with lots of irrelevant and low-quality posts to - I assume - attract "random" searchers and get more "affiliate clicks". But the interesting posts are still there, I just don't want my news-reader to be flooded with all the other noise. So I have tried creating a slimmed-down custom news-feed with only the "interesting" posts.

"My feed" is based on the official news feed, but I'm filtering it in an attempt to keep only the essential posts. Currently the feed skips posts in the categories Deal Zone, Buyers Guide, Smart Picks, Canon Reviews, Industry News and Industry Rumors. But I will adjust the filtering when I feel the need to do so. Canon Rumors Forum - Latest new threads

Unlike the official forum feed which contains every new comment posted in Canon Rumors Forum, this custom-feed tries keeping track of only the latest new threads (topics) created in the forum. This feed also tries to avoid the threads that are created to be "comment-sections" for news-posts on the main-site. While comment-threads for Craig's news-posts usually have a pattern easy to identify, it is harder with comment-threads for Richard's posts, so I am now ignoring all threads started by richard in the forum. A bit unfair to Richard, but I think it is pretty rare he starts other topics in the forum. The custom-made feed is available in both JSON Feed and RSS format:
